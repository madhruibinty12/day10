HYDERABAD

Hyderabad offers numerous attractions that are vibrant both during the day and at night. Daytime highlights include the iconic Charminar, Golconda Fort, and the Salar Jung Museum. At night, Charminar shines brightly, Hussain Sagar Lake is illuminated, and Shilparamam's night bazaar comes alive.
